About

Ryan King is a student of Computer Science at Boston University, pursuing a combined MS/BA degree. This blog will be a chronicle of the remainder of his journey, and hopefully share a few of the lessons he learns along the way. The name of this blog, Careless Lisper, is a play on the name of the Lisp family of programming languages and the classic 80s hit, Careless Whisper by Wham.
